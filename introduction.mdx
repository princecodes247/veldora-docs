## What is Veldora?

Veldora is a cutting-edge Software as a Service (SAAS) solution that empowers frontend developers and nocode enthusiasts to effortlessly handle form data without the need for a backend or traditional content management systems. It serves as your hassle-free, all-in-one data management companion for a wide range of small projects, including portfolios, job boards, roadmaps, and todo tasks.

## Getting Started

Veldora is designed to be easy to use and integrate into your projects. Follow the steps below to get started with Veldora in minutes.

- [Create a Veldora account](https://app.veldora.io/register) and log in to the dashboard.
- Create a new bucket and give it a name.
- Add a new form to your bucket and configure the fields.
- Copy the form's ID and paste it into your frontend project.
- Submit the form and view the data in your bucket.
